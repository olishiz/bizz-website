Profile & Objectives

Established in February 2020 following the Malaysia-Qatar Joint Trade Committee (JTC) meeting in Kuala Lumpur in October 2019.

Supported by the National Chamber of Commerce and Industry Of Malaysia (NCCIM), we aim to achieve 3 major goals which are listed below:

Solid Network

To create a network for ideas, opportunities, and personal contacts that will promote trade and investment between Malaysia and Qatar, thus supporting the full spectrum of members from large corporations to small and medium enterprises.

Sustainable Cooperation

To promote greater understanding between the private sectors of Malaysia and Qatar to in collaborating towards developing a sustainable future via trade and investments.

Strong Partner

To collaborate with government authorities, to foster and deepen harmonious economic relations between Malaysia and Qatar.
