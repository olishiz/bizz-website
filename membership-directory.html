MQJBC Directory

The MQJBC covers the 5 major sectors of the Malaysian economy, being Agriculture, Manufacturing, Oil Gas and Energy, Infrastructure, and Services. Representing over 52% of the workforce in Malaysia, the MQJBC are industry stewards in their respective fields and is your first point of reference to pursue trade and investments between Malaysia and Qatar.

MQJBC Members generate a combine output of approximately over 480.8 billion Ringgit.
