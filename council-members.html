MQBC Member Profile – Datuk Mohamed Razeek Md Hussain; Chairperson:

Member Profile

Datuk Razeek has 40 years of experience in the corporate, construction, and property industries. He began his carrier as a design engineer in a consulting firm in London in 1981. He returned to Malaysia and worked in an Engineering Consultant Firm. He joined Sime UEP Berhad in 1987. Subsequently, he joined Land and General Berhad in 1991 and stayed for 13 years, where he ultimately served as Executive Director.

He was next appointed as the Project Director with Eastern and Oriental Berhad in 2003, where he managed high-end real estate development and the first phase of the reclamation works at Seri Tanjung Pinang.

He then moved to Dubai and Cairo as DAMAC’s Senior Vice President for a year. He returned to Malaysia for Malaysian Resources Corporation Berhad as the Chief Operating Officer and was subsequently appointed Chief Executive Officer in 2009.

He was appointed Chief Operating Officer in DRB-HICOM Berhad in 2012, with a portfolio covering Services (Pos Malaysia, Waste Management, Vehicle Inspection, Logistics, Airport Services, Education), Property, and Defense. He ‘retired’ at the age of 60 in 2018 and joined Dhaya Maju Infrastructure (Asia) Sdn Bhd (DMIA).
